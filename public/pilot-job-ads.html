What you'll do

As a Pilot, you'll pick up and deliver errands while staying connected with customers and support.

  • Accept assigned errands
  • Confirm pickup & drop-off
  • Share live updates
  • Handle items with care

What we look for

Bring professionalism, a valid ID, and a dependable vehicle that matches the work you want to do.

  • Government ID
  • Driver License
  • Vehicle registration
  • Insurance (where required)

Perks & support

We help you stay on top of your jobs with in-app navigation, status tracking, and admin support.

  • Flexible schedules
  • Weekly earnings insights
  • Instant updates
  • Admin help desk

How to apply

Open the ErrandBridge app, switch to Pilot mode, and complete your profile. Upload your verification documents directly from your Pilot settings and wait for approval.
